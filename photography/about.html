Lock Buford
About the artist
Lock Buford has spent much of his life in pursuit of what he desires most. These desires have ebbed and flowed with time, but one thing is certain: this is a man who has always pursued greatness.

No one contests that Lock's work is stellar. The driving force, as he has been rumored to say, is that "No idea can restrain the stroner idea." In light of this philosophically intriguing concept, we see a glimpse into the psyche of one who holds ideas closely. Although an avid poet, Lock has shared very little of his work.

Gratefully, Lock Buford has chosen to share his ideas in the form of photography. May he continue to do so for many years to come.
